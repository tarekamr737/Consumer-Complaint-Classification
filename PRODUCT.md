# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Internal analysts and customer-support representatives use the tool while triaging a consumer complaint. They paste a narrative and need a reliable category recommendation to route the case to the correct department quickly.

## Product Purpose

Consumer Complaint Classification is an internal decision-support tool that predicts the most likely complaint category from a pasted narrative. Success means faster, more consistent routing while making the model's confidence and alternatives clear enough for the analyst to apply informed judgment.

## Brand Personality

Professional, modern, minimal. The interface should feel like a focused enterprise product: calm under time pressure, deliberate, and trustworthy without pretending to be a conversation or marketing experience.

## Anti-references

Do not resemble a chatbot, a flashy marketing site, a consumer social app, or an academic/demo notebook. Avoid ChatGPT-style conversational framing and decorative dashboard clichés.

## Design Principles

1. Put routing judgment first: the analyst's next decision is more important than visual novelty.
2. Make the prediction legible: category, confidence, and alternatives must be interpretable at a glance.
3. Preserve human control: model output supports routing, it does not imply certainty or replace review.
4. Keep the workspace calm: use a minimal, enterprise-grade hierarchy that rewards repeated use.
5. Design for clear operation: keyboard paths, responsive structure, and accessible states are core functionality.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Meet WCAG AA with high contrast, a color-blind-safe palette, keyboard access, visible focus indicators, responsive layout, and reduced-motion support. Icons never stand alone as meaning, and confidence is communicated with text as well as color.
